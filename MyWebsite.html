Touch Phone

The modern phone. A post-industrial result of the first ever iPhone invented by apple and introduced by Steve Jobs! Ever since its conception, many companies have modernized and improved upon it making manufacturing extremely cheap!

Li-ion Battery

The lithium ion battery. Invented by John B. Goodenough in the early 1980s, its the most revolutionary technology that has impacted every industry, household, and individual!!

3D Printer

Originally FDM 3D printing was invented by Scott Crump in the 1980's. So many patents where filed that technlogical advancements did no benefit general public, that is, until the Reprap movement by Adrian Bowyer when key patents expired in 2005.

Neural Networks

Originally invented by Frank Rosenblatt and further developed by Georffry Hinton, into what is now known as Deep Learning, has completely changed how modern business's operate due to its incredible list of applications.

CPU

Invented by Federico Faggin, the first commercial CPU was Intel 4004 and released in 1971. This small chip packs quite the logic processing punch!! Without it, demanding computations would not be possible!

WWW

Otherwise known as the World Wide Wed, was invented by Tim Burners-Lee while working at CERN, which at the time was the largest internet node in europe. He combined it with Hypertext and created the worlds first web server.
